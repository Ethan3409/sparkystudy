Frequently Asked Questions

What does the 7-day free trial include? ▼

The 7-day free trial gives you full access to all Elite features, including all modules, simulators, learning games, and tools. No credit card required. After 7 days, your trial ends and you'll be prompted to start a paid subscription.

How does GroupElite pricing work? ▼

GroupElite uses sliding scale pricing that decreases as your group grows. With 2 people, each pays $89/year. With 10+ people, each pays only $52/year. All members get full Elite features plus group collaboration tools. Use the slider above to see your exact price.

Can I switch plans after subscribing? ▼

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-month, we'll prorate the charge. If you downgrade, you'll receive a credit toward your next month.

What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept all major credit and debit cards: Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. All payments are processed securely with 256-bit SSL encryption and are PCI DSS compliant.

Do you offer annual discounts? ▼

All our plans are annual subscriptions with one fixed price per year. For GroupElite, larger groups automatically get better per-person pricing. We occasionally offer promotional codes for students and educational institutions.

Can I cancel anytime? ▼

Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. You'll retain access to your account and study progress through that date.
