Timers and Smart Relays

Rationale

Why is it important for you to learn this skill?

Electrical circuits are designed to control a wide variety of operations. Some of these
circuits require events to occur after a specific time has elapsed. With technology
improving and the world becoming increasingly automated, it is important that you are
aware of and understand some of the equipment that can provide timing control.

Outcome

When you have completed this module, you will be able to

Analyze timers and smart relays.

Objectives

1. Describe timers and basic timing functions.
2. Describe smart relays and basic timing functions.
3. Connect and analyze timers and smart relays.

NOTE
Objective Three is a practical objective and is not coveredin |
this module.
Introduction

This module describes different timing devices with emphasis on timing relays.
Different timing functions and some timing relay control circuits are illustrated.
The basic principles of smart relays are also examined.
