Relays and Contactors

Rationale

Why is it important for you to learn this skill?

Relays and contactors are used extensively in the electrical industry for various control
applications. To install and maintain them in good condition, you need to understand
their operation, be able to interpret their nameplate information, and recognize the

different types.

Outcome

When you have completed this module, you will be able to

Connect and analyze relays and contactors.

Objectives

1. Describe different types of relays and contactors.

2. Interpret nameplate information.

3. Identify the three main parts of a relay.

4. Describe the purpose of laminations and shading coils in relays and contactors.

5. Identify the applications, advantages, and disadvantages of typical materials used for
constructing contacts.

Identify the advantages and disadvantages of contact configurations.

Describe the operation of relays and contactors.

8. Connect and analyze relay and contactor operation.
