Rationale
Why is it important for you to learn this skill?
Asan electrician you will work with many different types of loads including several

types of inductors and capacitors. It is important to understand the basic properties of
these loads and the equipment that uses them before connecting them into a circuit.

Outcome
When you have completed this module, you will be able to

Describe the properties of inductors and capacitors.

Objectives

Describe an inductor (coil) and its characteristics.
Describe induction and its effects.

Describe inductance and the factors which affect it.
Describe capacitor construction, types, and applications.
Describe capacitance and the factors which affect it.

Pei adel dest

Introduction

This module introduces you to the basics of inductors (coils) and capacitors. You will
learn the different types of construction and how that can impact the circuit properties of
these devices. The basics of the many applications which inductors and capacitors can be
used for will also be covered in the module.
