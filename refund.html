Refund Policy

SparkStudy subscriptions are non-refundable except under specific circumstances. To be eligible for a refund, you must:

  • Be currently enrolled in an accredited electrical trade program or apprenticeship
  • Provide official documentation proving your enrollment (school schedule, acceptance letter, etc.)
  • Provide official grade documentation or proof of program withdrawal
  • Submit your request within 30 days of purchase
Important: Refund requests are reviewed within 5-10 business days. Approved refunds are processed to your original payment method within 7-14 additional business days.
